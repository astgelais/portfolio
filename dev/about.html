About Me

I'm a Paid Search Strategist, Developer, Affiliate Marketer, Ice Hockey Player, and Boston Sports Fan.

I have been involved in the online marketing industry since 2008, when I became an affiliate marketer promoting many different companies through a variety of online advertising mediums (SEM/PPC, SEO, Social Media, etc.). Since then, I have continued to learn and immerse myself into the rapidly changing market of the online marketing world. This fast paced industry keeps me on my toes, but is one of the things I love about working in this field.

I am well versed in online marketing and management of a company's online presence, and I have had the opportunity to share some of my knowledge as a consultant with different online companies. I am experienced in search engine marketing using many channels (Google AdWords, Bing Ads, and more), social media marketing, and coding (HTML, PHP, and MySQL).

Goals: I would like to continue working in the online industry as I believe it is a rapidly changing and challenging industry that has much more room to grow.

Specialties: Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), MySQL, PHP, JavaScript, HTML, WordPress, Affiliate/Performance Marketing, Website Optimization, Microsoft Excel, Google Analytics, Adsense, Adwords, BingAds
